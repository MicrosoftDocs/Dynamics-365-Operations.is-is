    > [!NOTE]
    > <span data-ttu-id="609ce-111">Sjálfgefna sýnin sem birtist þegar þú opnar **Eignamat** síðu fer eftir gildi sem er valið í **Skoða** reit á **Eignir** flipanum **Breytur eignastýringar** síðu (**Eignastýring** \> **Uppsetning** \> **Breytur eignastýringar**).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="609ce-111">The default view that is shown when you open the **Asset view** page depends on the value that is selected in the **View** field on the **Assets** tab of the **Asset management parameters** page (**Asset management** \> **Setup** \> **Asset management parameters**).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="609ce-112">Hægra megin á síðunni sýnir flýtiflipar upplýsingar um valinn skjá.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="609ce-112">On the right side of the page, FastTabs shows details of the selected view.</span></span>
