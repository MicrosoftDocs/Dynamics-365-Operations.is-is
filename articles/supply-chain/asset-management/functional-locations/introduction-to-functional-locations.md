[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

 

<span data-ttu-id="97c20-104">Þetta efni veitir yfirlit yfir virkar staðsetningar í eignastýringu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-104">This topic provides an overview of functional locations in Asset Management.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="97c20-105">Virkar staðsetningar eru þættir í tæknilegri uppbyggingu, svo sem virkar einingar í kerfi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-105">Functional locations are elements of a technical structure, such as the functional units in a system.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="97c20-106">Virkar staðsetningar eru búnar til með stigveldi og þú setur upp eignir á þeim.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-106">Functional locations are created hierarchically, and you install assets on them.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="97c20-107">Uppsetning virkra staðsetninga í fyrirtækinu þínu fer eftir kröfum fyrirtækisins.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-107">The setup of functional locations in your company depends on the company's requirements.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="97c20-108">Hér eru nokkur dæmi um hvernig þú getur notað virkar staðsetningar:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-108">Here are some examples of how you can use functional locations:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="97c20-109">**Virkt** - Virkar staðsetningar geta verið notendamiðaðar og notuð til að stjórna eignum sem hafa svipaða hegðun.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-109">**Functional** – The functional locations can be user-oriented and used to manage assets that have similar behavior.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="97c20-112">Hverri virkri staðsetningu er stjórnað sjálfstætt í eignastjórnun.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-112">Each functional location is managed independently in Asset Management.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="97c20-113">Hér eru sumar nytsamlegar aðgerðir virkra staðsetninga:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-113">Here are some of the useful features of functional locations:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="97c20-115">Settu upp kröfur um eignaupplýsingar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-115">Set up asset specification requirements.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="97c20-116">Settu upp viðhaldsraðir til fyrirbyggjandi og viðbragðs viðhalds.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-116">Set up maintenance sequences for preventive and reactive maintenance.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="97c20-117">Stýra uppsettar eignum.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-117">Manage installed assets.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="97c20-118">Fylgstu með virkum beiðnum og verkbeiðnum sem tengjast uppsettum eignum.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-118">Track active requests and work orders that are related to installed assets.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="97c20-119">Rektu galla sem eru skráðir á eignir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-119">Track faults that are registered on assets.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="97c20-120">Fylgstu með viðhaldskostnaði á eignunum sem tengjast virkri staðsetningu á hverjum tíma.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-120">Track maintenance costs on the assets that are related to a functional location at any given time.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="97c20-121">Virkar staðsetningar veita rekjanleika eigna í tengslum við beiðnir, verkbeiðnir, bilanaskráningar, ástandsmat, framleiðslu stöðvunarskráninga og skráningar eignateljara.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-121">Functional locations provide traceability of assets in relation to requests, work orders, fault registrations, condition assessments, production stop registrations, and asset counter registrations.</span></span>

> <span data-ttu-id="97c20-122">Jafnvel þó eign sé sett upp á ýmsum virkum staðsetningum á lífstímas sínum getur kostnaðurinn tengst hverjum stað.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-122">Even if an asset is installed on various functional locations during its lifetime, the costs can be related to each location.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="97c20-123">Með öðrum orðum, er eignakostnaður alltaf tengdur virku staðsetningunni sem eignin var sett upp á á hverjum tíma.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-123">In other words, asset costs are always related to the functional location that the asset was installed on at a given time.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="97c20-124">Virkar staðsetningar eru **ekki** sveigjanlegar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-124">Functional locations are **not** flexible.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="97c20-125">Þess vegna, eftir að þú hefur sett upp virkt staðsetningarstigveldi, geturðu ekki flutt staðsetningu í því.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97c20-125">Therefore, after you set up a functional location hierarchy, you can't move locations around in it.</span></span>
