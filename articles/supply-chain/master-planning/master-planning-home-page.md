# <a name="master-planning-home-page"></a><span data-ttu-id="57d11-103">Aðaláætlanagerð – heimasíða</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-103">Master planning home page</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="57d11-104">Í grunninn gerir Aðaláætlanagerð fyrirtækjum kleift að ákvarða og stilla af framtíðarþörf fyrir hráefni og afkastagetu til að mæta markmiðum fyrirtækisins.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-104">At its core, Master planning allows companies to determine and balance the future need for raw materials and capacity to meet company goals.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-105">Aðaláætlanagerð metur eftirfarandi:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-105">Master planning assesses the following:</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="57d11-106">Hvaða hráefni og afkastageta eru nú í boði?</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-106">What raw materials and capacities are currently available?</span></span> 
-  <span data-ttu-id="57d11-107">Hvaða hráefni og afkastageta eru nauðsynleg til að ljúka framleiðslu?</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-107">What raw materials and capacities are required to complete production?</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-108">Til dæmis, hvað verður að framleiða, kaupa, flytja eða setja til hliðar sem öryggisbirgðir áður en þú getur lokið framleiðslu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-108">For example, what must be manufactured, purchased, transferred, or set aside as safety stock before you can complete production.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="57d11-109">Aðaláætlanagerð notar upplýsingarnar til að reikna út kröfur og búa til áætlaðar pantanir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-109">Master planning uses the information to calculate the requirements and generate planned orders.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="57d11-110">Þrír helstu ferlar áætlanagerðar eru:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-110">The three main planning processes are:</span></span>

-  <span data-ttu-id="57d11-111">**Aðaláætlanagerð** - Aðaláætlunin reiknar út nettókröfur.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-111">**Master planning** - The Master plan calculates net requirements.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-112">Það er byggt á grundvelli raunverulegra núgildandi pantana og gerir fyrirtækjum kleift að stjórna birgðaáfyllingu á daglegum skammtímagrunni.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-112">It is based on actual current orders and enables companies to control inventory replenishment on a short-term, day-to-day basis.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-113">Annað hugtak til að lýsa því er *Nettókröfur áætlun*.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-113">Another term to describe it is the *Net requirements plan*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-114">Frekari upplýsingar, sjá [Aðaláætlanagerð](master-plans.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-114">For more information, see [Master planning](master-plans.md).</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="57d11-115">**Spáráætlanagerð** - Spáráætlunin reiknar út brúttókröfur.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-115">**Forecast planning** - The Forecast schedule calculates gross requirements.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-116">Það byggir á framreikningi til framtíðar (eða spám) og gerir fyrirtækjum kleift að gera langtíma áætlun fyrir efnisþarfir og afköst.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-116">It is based on future projections (or forecasts), and enables companies to conduct long-term planning of materials and capacity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-117">Frekari upplýsingar, sjá [Spáráætlanagerð](introduction-demand-forecasting.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-117">For more information, see [Forecast planning](introduction-demand-forecasting.md).</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="57d11-118">**Aðaláætlanagerð innan samsteypu** - Aðaláætlanagerð innan samsteypu reiknar nettókröfur yfir lögaðila.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-118">**Intercompany master planning** - The Intercompany master plan calculates net requirements across legal entities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-119">Það tengir eftirspurn og framboð milli fyrirtækja, ekki aðeins til skamms tíma, ákveðna eftirspurn og framboð heldur einnig til langs tíma, fyrirhuguð (það er ekki enn ákveðin) eftirspurn og framboð.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-119">It connects demand and supply between companies not only for short term, firm demand and supply but also for long-term, planned (that is not yet firmed) demand and supply.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-120">Frekari upplýsingar, sjá [Aðaláætlanagerð innan samsteypu](https://mbspartner.microsoft.com/AX/CourseOverview/1276) (eLearning) (krefst CustomerSource reiknings).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-120">For more information, see [Intercompany master planning](https://mbspartner.microsoft.com/AX/CourseOverview/1276)  (eLearning) (requires CustomerSource account).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="57d11-121">Fyrirtæki geta breytt úttaki áætlunarinnar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-121">Companies can change the output of the plan.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-122">Þeir geta keyrt endurnýjun, nettóbreyting eða báðir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-122">They can run regenerative, net change, or both.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-123">Endurnýjunaráætlanir uppfæra allar kröfur, en nettóbreytingaráætlanir uppfæra aðeins áætlunina varðandi atriði með nýjar kröfur sem hafa komið inn frá síðustu áætlunarkeyrslu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-123">Regenerative plans update all requirements, whereas, net change plans only update the plan on items with new requirements that have come in since the last scheduling run.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="57d11-124">Í aðaláætlunum er iðulega horft til skamms tíma, sem getur verið allt frá einni viku til sex mánaða.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-124">Master scheduling plans typically involve the short term, which can be anywhere from one week to six months.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-125">Kerfiseining aðaláætlunargerðar ákvarðar þær þarfir framboðs (efni) og getu (tilföng) sem munu uppfylla núverandi eftirspurn (nettókröfurnar).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-125">The Master planning module determines the supply (materials) and capacity (resources) needs that will meet current demand (the net requirements).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="57d11-126">Í flestum fyrirtækjum er þetta útvíkkað til að fela í sér lengsta uppsafnaða afhendingartíma meðal þeirra vara sem á að veita móttöku.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="57d11-126">In most companies this is extended to include the longest cumulative lead time among the products to be received.</span></span>
