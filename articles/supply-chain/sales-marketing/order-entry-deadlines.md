<span data-ttu-id="30986-107">Í mörgum fyrirtækjum verður sölupöntun að hafa borist fyrir ákveðinn tíma dags til þess að hún sé unninn eins og hún hafi borist þann dag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30986-107">In many companies, only sales orders that are received before a certain time of day are treated as if they were received on that day.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30986-108">Allar pantanir sem er tekið eftir þann dag eru meðhöndlaðar eins og ef þær eru mótteknar í næsta virka dag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30986-108">Any orders that are received after that time are treated as if they are received on the next business day.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30986-109">Þessi loktími fyrir pantanir kallast lokadagur fyrir skráningu pantana.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30986-109">This cut-off time for orders is known as the order entry deadline.</span></span>
