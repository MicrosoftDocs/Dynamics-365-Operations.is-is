[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="38151-104">Þetta ferli fer í gegnum skilgreining tengdra afurða.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-104">This procedure walks through configuring linked products.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38151-105">Þetta ferli notar sýnigögn USRT og gerir ráð fyrir bæði vörur hafa þegar verið stofnuð og losuð.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-105">This procedure uses the USRT demo data, and assumes both products have already been created and released.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="38151-106">Fara í upplýsingar um afurðastjórnun > Afurðir > Útgefnar afurðir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-106">Go to Product information management > Products > Released products.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="38151-107">Í listanum skal finna og velja þá skráningu sem óskað er eftir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-107">In the list, find and select the desired record.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="38151-108">Í aðgerðasvæðinu er smellt á Retail og Commerce.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-108">On the Action Pane, click Retail and Commerce.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="38151-109">Smellt er á Tengdar afurðir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-109">Click Linked products.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="38151-110">Smella á Breyta.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-110">Click Edit.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="38151-111">Í reitnum númer tengdrar afurðar skal smella á fellilistahnappinn til að opna leitina.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-111">In the Linked product number field, click the drop-down button to open the lookup.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="38151-112">Í listanum skal smella á tengilinn í valinni línu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-112">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="38151-113">Í reitnum Eining skal smella á fellilistahnappinn til að opna leitina.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-113">In the Unit field, click the drop-down button to open the lookup.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="38151-114">Í listanum skal smella á tengilinn í valinni línu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-114">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="38151-115">Færið inn númer í reitnum „Magn“.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-115">In the Quantity field, enter a number.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="38151-116">Smellið á „Vista“.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38151-116">Click Save.</span></span>
