[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="8b8d6-104">Þjónustusamningsflokkar eru gagnlegir til að flokka og sía þjónustusamninga.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b8d6-104">Service agreement groups are useful for sorting and filtering service agreements.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="8b8d6-105">Smellið á **Þjónustustjórnun** \> **Uppsetningu** \> **Þjónustusamningar** \> **Þjónustusamningsflokkar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b8d6-105">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreement groups**.</span></span>
