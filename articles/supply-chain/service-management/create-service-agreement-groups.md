# <a name="create-service-agreement-groups"></a><span data-ttu-id="71b36-103">Stofna þjónustusamningsflokka</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71b36-103">Create service agreement groups</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="71b36-104">Þjónustusamningsflokkar eru gagnlegir til að flokka og sía þjónustusamninga.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71b36-104">Service agreement groups are useful for sorting and filtering service agreements.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="71b36-105">Smellið á **Þjónustustjórnun** \> **Uppsetningu** \> **Þjónustusamningar** \> **Þjónustusamningsflokkar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71b36-105">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreement groups**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="71b36-106">Ýtið á **Alt+N** til að stofna nýjan þjónustusamningsflokk.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71b36-106">Press **Alt+N** to create a new service agreement group.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="71b36-107">Færið inn auðkenni þjónustusamningsflokks í reitinn **Þjónustusamningsflokkur**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71b36-107">In the **Service agreement group** field, enter an ID for the service agreement group.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="71b36-108">Í reitnum **Lýsing** er færð inn lýsing á þjónustusamningsflokknum.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71b36-108">In the **Description** field, enter a description for the service agreement group.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="71b36-109">Lokið síðunni til að vista breytingarnar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71b36-109">Close the page to save your changes.</span></span>

> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="71b36-110">Ef þjónustusamningsflokkur er tilgreindur í reitnum **Þjónustusamningsflokkur** á síðunni **Færibreytur þjónustustjórnunar** verður mælt með þessum flokki fyrir alla nýja þjónustusamninga sem notandinn stofnar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71b36-110">If a service agreement group is defined in the **Service agreement group** field of the **Service management parameters** page, the program suggests this group for all new service agreements that you create.</span></span>
