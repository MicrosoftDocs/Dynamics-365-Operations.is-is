[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

1. <span data-ttu-id="c2d1f-105">Smellið á **Þjónustustjórnun** \> **Uppsetning** \> **Þjónustusamningar** \> **Tímagluggi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2d1f-105">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service agreements** \> **Time window**.</span></span>
