# <a name="delete-a-service-bom"></a><span data-ttu-id="e6599-103">Eyða þjónustuuppskrift</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e6599-103">Delete a service BOM</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="e6599-104">Smellið á **Þjónustustjórnun** \> **Almennt** \> **Þjónustusamningar** \> **þjónustusamningar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e6599-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e6599-105">Veljið þjónustusamninginn sem á að eyða þjónustuuppskrift (BOM) úr.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e6599-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="e6599-106">Á **Aðgerðarúða**, smelltu á **Þjónustuhlutir**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e6599-106">On the **Action Pane**, click **Service objects**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="e6599-107">Í **Þjónustuhlutir** skjámyndinni skaltu velja þjónustuhlut sem þú vilt eyða þjónustuuppskrift frá.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e6599-107">In the **Service objects** form, select the service object that you want to delete a service BOM from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e6599-108">Smelltu á **Aðgerðir** og veldu síðan **Eyða þjónustuuppskrift**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e6599-108">Click **Functions**, and then select **Delete service BOM**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="e6599-109">Smellið á **Já**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e6599-109">Click **Yes**.</span></span>


> [!NOTE]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="e6599-110">Ef þú hefur ekki úthlutað sniðmátsuppskrift getur þjónustuuppskriftinni verið eytt úr <STRONG>Sniðmátsuppskrift</STRONG> skjámyndinni.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e6599-110">If you have not applied a template BOM, the service BOM can be deleted from the <STRONG>Template BOMs</STRONG> form.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e6599-111">Ef sniðmátsuppskriftin var hins vegar notuð, er ekki hægt að eyða þjónustuuppskriftinni með þessari aðferð.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e6599-111">If you have applied a template BOM, the service BOM cannot be deleted by using this method.</span></span></P>
