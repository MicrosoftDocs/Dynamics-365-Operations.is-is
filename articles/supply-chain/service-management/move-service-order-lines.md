# <a name="move-service-order-lines"></a><span data-ttu-id="8c6dc-103">Færa þjónustupöntunarlínur</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8c6dc-103">Move service order lines</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="8c6dc-104">Smelltu á **Þjónustustjórnun** \> **Almennt** \> **Þjónustupantanir** \> **Þjónustupantanir**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8c6dc-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service orders** \> **Service orders**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="8c6dc-105">Tvísmelltu á þjónustupöntunina sem inniheldur línurnar sem þú vilt flytja í aðra þjónustupöntun.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8c6dc-105">Double-click the service order that contains the lines that you want to move to another service order.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="8c6dc-106">Í skjámyndinni **Þjónustupantanir**, í aðgerðasvæðinu, skal smella á hnappinn **Færa línur**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8c6dc-106">In the **Service orders** form, on the Action Pane, click the **Move lines** button.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="8c6dc-107">Í skjámyndinni **Færa þjónustupöntunarlínur**, í reitnum **Þjónustupöntun**, skal velja þjónustupöntunina sem þú vilt flytja línurnar til.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8c6dc-107">In the **Move service order lines** form, in the **Service order** field, select the service order that you want to move the lines to.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="8c6dc-108">Veldu gátreitinn **Eyða tómri þjónustupöntun** til að eyða þjónustupöntuninni sem þú fluttir þjónustupöntunarlínurnar úr.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8c6dc-108">Select the **Delete empty service order** check box to delete the service order that you moved the service order lines from.</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="8c6dc-109">Smellið á **Í lagi**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8c6dc-109">Click **OK**.</span></span>


> [!NOTE]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="8c6dc-110">Þú getur aðeins flutt þjónustupöntunarlínur milli þjónustupantana sem eru hengdar við sama þjónustusamninginn.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8c6dc-110">You can move service order lines only between service orders that are attached to the same service agreement.</span></span></P>
