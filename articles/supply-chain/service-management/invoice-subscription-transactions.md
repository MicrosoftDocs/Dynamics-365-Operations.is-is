1.  <span data-ttu-id="1b7ca-104">Smelltu á **Þjónustustjórnun** \> **Almennt** \> **Þjónustuáskriftir** \> **Allar þjónustuáskriftir**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1b7ca-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>
