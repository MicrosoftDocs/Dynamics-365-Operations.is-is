[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="1d52d-104">Veljið **Þjónustustjórnun** \> **Uppsetning** \> **Þjónusta áskrift** \> **Áskriftarflokkar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1d52d-104">Go to **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **Subscription groups**.</span></span>
