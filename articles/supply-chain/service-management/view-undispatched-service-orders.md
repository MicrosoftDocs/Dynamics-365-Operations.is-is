# <a name="view-undispatched-service-orders"></a><span data-ttu-id="8cc21-103">Skoða ósendar þjónustupantanir</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8cc21-103">View undispatched service orders</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="8cc21-104">Þetta efnisatriði lýsir hvernig á að skoða verkþætti í þjónustupöntun sem hafa ekki starfsmann sem þeim er úthlutað úr við sendingarhóp.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8cc21-104">This topic describes how to view activities in a service order that don’t have a worker assigned to them from a dispatch team.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="8cc21-105">Smelltu á **Þjónustustjórnun** \> **Reglubundið** \> **Sendingartafla**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8cc21-105">Click **Service management** \> **Periodic** \> **Dispatch board**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="8cc21-106">Í glugganum **Sendingartafla** skal færa inn upphafsdagsetningu og lokadagsetningu fyrir tímabilið þar sem sendingarverkþættir eru skoðaðir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8cc21-106">In the **Dispatch board** window, enter the start date and end date for the period to view dispatch activity for.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="8cc21-107">Valfrjálst: Velja hvort hafa eigi lokaða verkþætti og tengda hópa í niðurstöðunum.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8cc21-107">Optional: Select whether to include closed activities and related teams in the results.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="8cc21-108">Smellið á **Í lagi** til að skoða niðurstöðurnar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8cc21-108">Click **OK** to view the results.</span></span>
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P><span data-ttu-id="8cc21-109">Í skjámyndinni <STRONG>Sendingartafla</STRONG> birtist yfirlitið <STRONG>Ítarlegt</STRONG> sjálfkrafa.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8cc21-109">In the <STRONG>Dispatch board</STRONG> form, the <STRONG>Advanced</STRONG> view is automatically displayed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8cc21-110">Í þessari sýn eru Gantt-ritið efst á blaðsíðunni og fliparnir neðst á síðunni birtir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8cc21-110">In this view, the Gantt chart at the top of the page and the tabs at the bottom of the page are displayed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8cc21-111">Smelltu á <STRONG>Einfalt</STRONG> til að sýna aðeins Gantt-ritið.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8cc21-111">Click <STRONG>Simple</STRONG> to display only the Gantt chart.</span></span></P>



5.  <span data-ttu-id="8cc21-112">Til að skoða lista yfir þjónustupantanir sem hafa ekki úthlutaðan sendingarhóp skal smella á flipann **Ósent**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8cc21-112">To view a list of service orders that don’t have a dispatch team assigned, click the **Undispatched** tab.</span></span>
