[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="1f987-104">Þjónustubil gefur til kynna þá tíðni sem þjónustupöntunarlínur eru stofnaðar fyrir þjónustusamningslínur þegar þjónustupantanir eru stofnaðar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1f987-104">Service interval indicates the frequency with which service order lines are created for service agreement lines when you create service orders.</span></span>
