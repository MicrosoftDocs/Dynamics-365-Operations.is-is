[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="2e21b-104">Þjónustubil gefur til kynna þá tíðni sem þjónustupöntunarlínur eru stofnaðar fyrir þjónustusamningslínur þegar þjónustupantanir eru stofnaðar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2e21b-104">Service interval indicates the frequency with which service order lines are created for service agreement lines when you create service orders.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="2e21b-105">Smellið á **Þjónustustjórnun** \> **Uppsetning** \> **Þjónustusamningar** \> **Þjónustubil**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2e21b-105">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service intervals**.</span></span>
