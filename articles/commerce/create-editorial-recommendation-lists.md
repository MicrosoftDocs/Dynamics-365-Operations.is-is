## <a name="create-a-new-list"></a><span data-ttu-id="a4ce2-106">Stofnið nýjan lista.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4ce2-106">Create a new list</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="a4ce2-108">Farðu í **Retail og Commerce &gt; Afurðatillögur &gt; Tillögulistar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4ce2-108">Go to **Retail and Commerce &gt; Product recommendations &gt; Recommendation lists**.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="a4ce2-109">Veljið **Nýtt**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4ce2-109">Select **New**.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="a4ce2-110">Í reitinn **Listakenni** skal slá inn gildi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4ce2-110">In the **List Id** field, enter a value.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="a4ce2-111">Í reitinn **Listaheiti** skal slá inn gildi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4ce2-111">In the **List name** field, enter a value.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="a4ce2-112">**Listaheiti** er titill listans sem mun birtast í samanburðarlistanum í einingunni **Vörusafn**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4ce2-112">The **List name** is the title of the list that will appear in the curated lists section of the **Product collection** module.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="a4ce2-114">Til að breyta röð á vörum á listanum, sláðu inn gildi í dálkinum **Sýna röðun**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4ce2-114">To change the order of the products in the list, enter a value in the **Display order** column.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="a4ce2-115">Ef tvær vörur hafa sama gildi skjáraðar, þá getur endanleg röð þessara tveggja niðurstaðna verið frábrugðin bakforritinu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4ce2-115">If two products have the same display order value, then the final order of those two results may differ from the back office.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="a4ce2-116">Veldu **Vista** til að vista listann.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4ce2-116">Select **Save** to save the list.</span></span>
