# <a name="letters-of-credit"></a><span data-ttu-id="d37a0-103">Kreditbréf</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-103">Letters of credit</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="d37a0-104">Kreditbréf eru bankaskjöl sem eru almennt notuð fyrir kaup og sölu á vörum yfir alþjóðleg landamæri.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-104">Letters of credit are bank documents that are commonly used for the purchase and sale of goods across international borders.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="d37a0-105">Kreditbréf eru notuð fyrir alþjóðlegar færslur til að tryggja að greiðslur verða greiddar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-105">Letters of credit are used for international transactions to ensure that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d37a0-106">Skjalaupplýsingar um kreditbréf er samning sem er gefið út af banka, sem bankinn samþykkir til að tryggja að greiðslur fyrir hönd kaupanda, ef skilmálum samningsins milli seljanda og kaupanda eru uppfyllt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-106">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to ensure payment on behalf of the buyer, if the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d37a0-107">Athugið að einnig er vísað til kreditbréfs sem viðskiptaskjals (DC).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-107">Note that a letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="d37a0-108">Við innflutning kreditbréfs er lögaðili kaupandi eða umsækjandi um kreditbréf.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-108">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d37a0-109">Við útflutning kreditbréfs er lögaðili seljandi eða rétthafi kreditbréfs.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-109">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d37a0-110">Eftirfarandi aðilar koma að kreditbréfi:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-110">The following parties are involved with a letter of credit:</span></span> 

 - <span data-ttu-id="d37a0-111">Umsækjandi (kaupandi) sem ætlar að greiða fyrir vörur</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-111">The applicant (buyer) who intends to pay for the goods</span></span> 
 - <span data-ttu-id="d37a0-112">Rétthafi (seljandi) sem tekur við greiðslunni</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-112">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="d37a0-113">Útgáfubankinn sem gefur út kreditbréf</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-113">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="d37a0-114">Ráðgefandi banki sem sér um færsluna fyrir hönd umsækjanda</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-114">The advising bank that carries out the transaction on behalf of the applicant</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d37a0-115">Kreditbréf inniheldur lýsingu á vörum, þau skjöl sem krafist er, dagsetningu sendingar og lokadagsetningu sem greiðslur verða ekki gerðar eftir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-115">The letter of credit includes a description of the goods, any required documents, the date of shipment, and the expiration date after which payment will not be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d37a0-116">Útgáfubankinn rukkar hlutfall fyrir kreditbréf.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-116">The issuing bank collects a margin for the letter of credit.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="d37a0-117">Kreditbréf getur annað hvort verið afturkallanlegt eða óafturkallanlegt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-117">A letter of credit can be revocable or irrevocable.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d37a0-118">Eðli kreditbréfs getur verið framseljanlegt, ekki framseljanlegt, eða reikningslán.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-118">The nature of a letter of credit can be transferable, non transferable, or revolving.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d37a0-119">Yfirleitt er kreditbréf óafturkallanlegur og staðfestur samningur um að greiðsla berist tilteknum rétthafa við afhendingu fullra og réttra afhendingarskjala.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d37a0-119">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>
