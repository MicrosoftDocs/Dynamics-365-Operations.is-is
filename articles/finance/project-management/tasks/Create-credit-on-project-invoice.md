<span data-ttu-id="05e60-116">Eftir þetta skref mun kreditnóta vera aðgengileg fyrir reikningstillögu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="05e60-116">After this step, the Credit Note will be available for Invoice Proposal.</span></span>
