11. <span data-ttu-id="4cf4d-126">Færið inn kenni í reitnum **Greiðsluskilmálar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4cf4d-126">In the **Terms of payment field**, enter an ID.</span></span>
