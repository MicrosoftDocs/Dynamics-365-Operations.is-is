# <a name="create-a-legal-entity"></a><span data-ttu-id="248a9-103">Stofna lögaðila</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-103">Create a legal entity</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="248a9-104">Lögaðili er fyrirtæki sem er auðkennt með skráningu til löggiltra yfirvalda.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-104">A legal entity is an organization that is identified through registration with a legal authority.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-105">Lögaðila er hægt að færa inn í samninga og eru þeir krafnir um að útbúa yfirlit sem segir til um frammistöðu þeirra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-105">Legal entities can enter into contracts and are required to prepare statements that report on their performance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-106">Notið eftirfarandi aðferðir til þess að stofna lögaðili.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-106">The following procedure explains how to create a legal entity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-107">Sýnigögn fyrirtækisins til að stofna þetta ferli er USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-107">The demo data company used to create this procedure is USMF.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-108">Farðu í **Skoðunarrúðu > Kerfiseiningar > Fyrirtækisstjórnun > Fyrirtæki > Lögaðilar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-108">Go to **Navigation pane > Modules > Organization administration > Organizations > Legal entities**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-109">Smellt er á **Nýtt**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-109">Click **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-110">Í reitinn **Heiti** skal slá inn gildi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-110">In the **Name** field, type a value.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-111">Í reitinn **Fyrirtæki** skal slá inn gildi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-111">In the **Company** field, type a value.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-112">Í reitinn **Land/svæði** skal slá inn eða velja gildi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-112">In the **Country/region** field, enter or select a value.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-113">Smellt er á **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-113">Click **OK**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-114">Í hlutanum **Almennt** skal veita eftirfarandi almennar upplýsingar um lögaðila: Færa inn leitarheiti ef leitarnafn er krafist.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-114">In the **General** section, provide the following general information about the legal entity: Enter a search name, if a search name is required.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-115">Leitarheiti er annað nafn sem hægt er að nota til að leita að þessum lögaðila.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-115">A search name is an alternate name that can be used to search for this legal entity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-116">Veljið hvort þessi lögaðili er notaður sem samstæðufyrirtæki.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-116">Select whether this legal entity is being used as a consolidation company.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-117">Veljið hvort þessi lögaðili er notaður sem losunarfyrirtæki.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-117">Select whether this legal entity is being used as an elimination company.</span></span> 
7. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-118">Útvíkkaðu hlutann **Aðsetur**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-118">Expand the **Addresses** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-119">Í hlutanum **Aðsetur** skal smella á **Breyta** til að færa inn upplýsingar um aðsetur, til dæmis götunafn og götunúmer, póstnúmer og borg.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-119">In the **Addresses** section, click **Edit** to enter address information, such as the street name and number, postal code, and city.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-120">Útvíkkaðu kaflann **Tengslaupplýsingar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-120">Expand the **Contact information** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-121">Í hlutanum **Tengslaupplýsingar** skal færa inn upplýsingar um aðferðir samskipta, svo sem tölvupósta, vefslóðir og símanúmer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-121">In the **Contact information** section, enter information about methods of communication, such as email addresses, URLs, and telephone numbers.</span></span> 
9. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-122">Útvíkkaðu hlutann **Lögboðin skýrslugerð**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-122">Expand the **Statutory reporting** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-123">Í hlutanum **Lögboðin skýrslugerð** skal færa inn skráningarnúmerin sem eru notuð fyrir lögboðna skýrslugerð.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-123">In the **Statutory reporting** section, enter the registration numbers that are used for statutory reporting.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-124">Útvíkkaðu hlutann **Skráningarnúmer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-124">Expand the **Registration numbers** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-125">Í hlutanum **Skráningarnúmer** skaltu slá inn allar upplýsingar sem lögaðilinn krefst.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-125">In the **Registration numbers** section, enter any information required by the legal entity.</span></span>  
11. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-126">Útvíkkaðu hlutann **Bankareikningsupplýsingar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-126">Expand the **Bank account information** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-127">Í hlutanum **Bankareikningsupplýsingar** skal slá inn bankareikninga og leiðarnúmer fyrir lögaðilann.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-127">In the **Bank account information** section, enter bank accounts and routing numbers for the legal entity.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-128">Útvíkkaðu hlutann **Erlend viðskipti og vörustjórnun**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-128">Expand the **Foreign trade and logistics** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-129">Í hlutanum **Erlend viðskipti og vörustjórnun** skaltu færa sendingarupplýsingar inn fyrir lögaðilann.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-129">In the **Foreign trade and logistics** section, enter shipping information for the legal entity.</span></span>  
13. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-130">Útvíkkaðu hlutann **Númeraröð**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-130">Expand the **Number sequences** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-131">Í hlutanum **Númeraröð** er hægt að skoða númeraraðirnar sem tengjast lögaðilanum.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-131">In the **Number sequences** section, you can view the number sequences that are associated with the legal entity.</span></span>  
14. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-132">Útvíkkaðu hlutann **Myndir**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-132">Expand the **Images** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-133">Í hlutanum **Myndir** skaltu skoða eða breyta merki og/eða mælaborð mynd sem eru tengdar við lögaðilann.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-133">In the **Images** section, view or change the logo and/or dashboard image that are associated with the legal entity.</span></span>  
15. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-134">Útvíkkaðu hlutann **Skattskráning**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-134">Expand the **Tax registration** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-135">Í hlutanum **Skattskráning** skal færa inn skráningarnúmerin sem eru notuð til að senda skýrslu til skattyfirvalda.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-135">In the **Tax registration** section, enter the registration numbers that are used to report to tax authorities.</span></span>
16. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-136">Útvíkkaðu hlutann **Skattur 1099**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-136">Expand the **Tax 1099** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="248a9-137">Í hlutanum **Skattur 1099** skal færa inn 1099 upplýsingar fyrir lögaðilann.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-137">In the **Tax 1099** section, enter 1099 information for the legal entity.</span></span>  
17. <span data-ttu-id="248a9-138">Smellt er á **Vista**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="248a9-138">Click **Save**.</span></span>
