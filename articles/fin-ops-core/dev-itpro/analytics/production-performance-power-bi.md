<span data-ttu-id="448b2-107">**Kostnaðarstjórnun** Power BI-efni er ætlað fyrir bókhaldara birgða eða einstaklinga innan fyrirtækisins sem bera ábyrgð á birgðum.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="448b2-107">The **Production performance** Power BI content is for production managers or individuals in the organization who are responsible for production control.</span></span>
