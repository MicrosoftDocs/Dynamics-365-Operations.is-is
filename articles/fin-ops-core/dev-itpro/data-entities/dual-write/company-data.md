[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="71f28-107">Common Data Service er ekki með sambærilegt hugtak.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71f28-107">Common Data Service doesn't have an equivalent concept.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="71f28-108">Næsta hugtakið er *rekstrareining*, sem er fyrst og fremst öryggis- og sýnileikamörk fyrir notendagögn.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71f28-108">The closest concept is *business unit*, which is primarily a security and visibility boundary for user data.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="71f28-109">Þetta hugtak hefur ekki sömu lagalegu eða viðskiptalegu afleiðingar og hugtakið fyrirtæki.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71f28-109">This concept doesn't have the same legal or business implications as the company concept.</span></span>

+ <span data-ttu-id="71f28-115">Þegar cdm\_Fyrirtækjaskrá er búin til og virkjuð fyrir tvískipt skrif, er sjálfgefin rekstrareining búin til með sama nafn.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71f28-115">When a cdm\_Company record is created and enabled for dual-write, a default business unit is created that has the same name.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="71f28-116">Þó að sjálfgefið teymi sé sjálfkrafa búið til fyrir þá rekstrareiningu er rekstrareiningin ekki notuð.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71f28-116">Although a default team is automatically created for that business unit, the business unit isn't used.</span></span>
+ <span data-ttu-id="71f28-117">Sérstakt eigendateymi er búið til sem hefur sama nafn.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71f28-117">A separate owner team is created that has the same name.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="71f28-118">Það er líka tengt rekstrareiningunni.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="71f28-118">It's also associated with the business unit.</span></span>
