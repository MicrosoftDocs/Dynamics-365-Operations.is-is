[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="81c41-104">Stofna greiðsluþóknanir fyrir greiðsluar viðskiptavinar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-104">Create payment fees for customer payments.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="81c41-105">Þetta verkefni notar USMF-sýnifyrirtækið.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-105">This task uses the USMF demo company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-106">Í **skoðunarrúðunni** ferðu í **Kerfi > Viðskiptakröfur > Greiðsluuppsetning > Greiðsluþóknun**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-106">In the **Navigation pane**, go to **Modules > Accounts receivable > Payments setup > Payment fee**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-107">Smellt er á **Nýtt**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-107">Click **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-108">Í reitinn **Kenni þóknunar** skal færa inn kenni þóknunar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-108">In the **Fee ID** field, enter a Fee ID.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-109">Kenni Þóknunar Birtist í greiðslubókum, þannig að hafðu það lýsandi til að skilja er hvaða þóknun er metin.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-109">The Fee ID displays on payment journals, so make it descriptive to understand what fee is being assessed.</span></span>  
4. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-110">Færa skal inn heiti þóknunar í reitinn **Heiti**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-110">In the **Name** field, enter a fee name.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-111">Færið inn lýsingu fyrir þóknun í reitinn **Lýsing þóknunar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-111">In the **Fee description** field, enter a description for the fee.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-112">Í reitnum **Gjald** skal velja hvort gjaldið verður skuldfært á viðskiptavin eða fjárhagslykil.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-112">In the **Charge** field, select whether the fee will be charged to the Customer or a Ledger account.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-113">Ef viðskiptavinur er metin þóknun, veljið Viðskiptavinar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-113">If the customer is assessed the fee, select Customer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-114">Ef þóknunin verður meta sem útgjöld á fyrirtækið , velja Fjárhag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-114">If the fee will be assess to your organization as an expense, select Ledger.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-115">Veljið Viðskiptavin fyrir þetta verk.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-115">For this task, select Customer.</span></span>  
7. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-116">Í reitnum **Gerð færslubókar** skal velja þá tegund færslubókar sem getur notað þetta greiðslugjald.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-116">In the **Journal type** field, select the type of journal that can use this payment fee.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-117">Ef þessi gjöld eru notaðar fyrir greiðslur viðskiptavina, færslubókargerð líklega verður greiðslu Viðskiptavinar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-117">If these fees are used for customer payments, the journal type will likely be Customer payment.</span></span>  
8. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-118">Smellt er á **Vista**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-118">Click **Save**.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-119">Smellt er á **Uppsetning greiðsluþóknunar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-119">Click **Payment fee setup**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-120">Uppsetning greiðsluþóknunar er notuð til að skilgreina skilyrði fyrir það hvenær greiðsluþóknun verður metin.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-120">The Payment fee setup is used to define the criteria for when the payment fee will be assessed.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="81c41-121">Til dæmis er hægt að skilgreina þóknunin verður reiknuð ef bankareikningurinn er USMF OPER og greiðsluháttur er ávísun.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-121">For example, you can define that the fee will be calculated if the bank account is USMF OPER, and the method of payment is check.</span></span>  
10. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-122">Í reitnum **Flokkanir** skal velja annaðhvort Töflu, Flokk eða Allt til að skilgreina hvaða bankareikningar þetta gjald verður skuldfært á.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-122">In the **Groupings** field, select either Table, Group or All to define which bank accounts will be assessed this fee.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-123">Ef Allt er valið, gæti allar bankareikningar verið metnar fyrir þessu gjaldi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-123">If you select All, all bank accounts could be assessed this fee.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="81c41-124">Ef Tafla er valinn er aðeins bankareikningur sem þú valdir verið metnar gjaldi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-124">If you select Table, only the bank account you select could be assessed this fee.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-125">Ef þú velur Flokk, einungis bankareikningur í valda bankaflokkinn gæti verið metnar þessu gjaldi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-125">If you select Group, only the bank accounts in the selected bank group could be assessed this fee.</span></span>  
11. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-126">Í reitnum **Bankavensl** skal velja annaðhvort bankaflokk eða bankareikning.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-126">In the **Bank relation** field, select either a bank group or a bank account.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-127">Ef Tafla er valin, birtir leitin bankareikninga.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-127">If you selected Table, the lookup will display bank accounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-128">Ef flokkur er valin, birtir leitin bankaflokka.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-128">If you selected Group, the lookup will display bank groups.</span></span>  
12. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-129">Í listanum skal smella á tengilinn í valinni línu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-129">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-130">Í reitnum **Greiðslumáti** skal velja þann greiðslumáta sem þetta gjald verður metið fyrir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-130">In the **Mehtod of payment** field, select the Method of payment for which this fee will be assessed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-131">Til dæmis er hægt að meta þóknun viðskiptavinum ef þeir senda greiðslur sem ávísun, frekar en sem rafræna greiðslu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-131">For example, you may assess a fee to your customers if they send payments as a check, rather than as an electronic payment.</span></span>  
14. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-132">Í listanum skal finna og velja þá skráningu sem óskað er eftir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-132">In the list, find and select the desired record.</span></span>
15. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-133">Ef það á við skal í reitnum **Greiðslugjaldmiðill** slá inn greiðslugjaldmiðil.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-133">If relevant, in the **Payment currency** field, enter a payment currency.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-134">Greiðslugjaldmiðli er notað sem aukaleg skilyrði fyrir hvort þóknunin verður metin.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-134">The payment currency is used as an additional criteria for whether the fee will be assessed.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="81c41-135">Til dæmis bankanum gæti gjaldfærð aukalegar þóknun fyrir greiðslur mótteknar í USD-gjaldmiðli, þar sem þær vanalega aðeins færðar í gjaldmiðli EUR.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-135">For example, your bank may charge an extra fee for payments received in USD currency, since they normally only transact in EUR currency.</span></span>  
16. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-136">Velja hvort gjaldið verður prósentu, upphæð eða bil.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-136">Select whether the fee will be a percent, amount or interval.</span></span>

18. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-141">Veljið gjaldmiðil fyrir þóknunina í reitnum **Gjaldmiðill þóknunar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-141">In the **Fee currency** field, select the currency of the fee.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="81c41-142">Þetta er gjaldmiðill sem þóknunin verður stofnuð í.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-142">This is the currency in which the fee will be created.</span></span>  
19. <span data-ttu-id="81c41-143">Smellt er á **Vista**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="81c41-143">Click **Save**.</span></span>
