[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

1. <span data-ttu-id="beb5e-106">Í skoðunarrúðunni ferðu í **Kerfiseiningar > Fjárhag > Bókhaldslykill > Víddir > Sjálfgefin sniðmát fjárhagsvídda**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="beb5e-106">In the navigation pane, go to **Modules > General ledger > Chart of accounts > Dimensions > Financial dimension default templates**.</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="beb5e-112">Færa inn aðra prósentu fyrir næstu víddargildi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="beb5e-112">Enter another percent for the next dimension values.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="beb5e-113">Í þessu dæmi skal færa inn prósentu svo þú sért enn með lægra en 100% úthlutað.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="beb5e-113">For this example, enter a percentage so you still have less than 100% allocated.</span></span>

9. <span data-ttu-id="beb5e-115">Veljið **Bæta við**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="beb5e-115">Select **Add**.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="beb5e-116">Færið inn aðra prósentu sem munu gera heildarprósenta jafnt og 100%.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="beb5e-116">Enter another percent, which will make the total percent equal to 100%.</span></span>
