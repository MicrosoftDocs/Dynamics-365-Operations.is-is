[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

3. <span data-ttu-id="c3937-111">Í reitinn Heiti skal slá inn gildi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c3937-111">In the Name field, type a value.</span></span>
