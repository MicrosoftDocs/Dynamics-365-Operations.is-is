[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="7e431-104">Kostnaðarbókhald fjárhags sýnir skráningareininguna í heild.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e431-104">A cost accounting ledger represents the overall reporting unit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e431-105">Það er skilgreint út frá kostnaðareiningarvídd, tölfræðilegri vídd, fjárhagsdagatali og gjaldmiðli.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e431-105">It is defined by a cost element dimension, statistical dimension, fiscal calendar, and currency.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e431-106">Í því felast efasemdir um hugtakið lögaðilar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e431-106">It is agnostic to the concept of legal entities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e431-107">Lögaðili og gögn hans geta tengst margvíslegu kostnaðarbókhaldi fjárhags.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e431-107">A legal entity and its data can be associated with many cost accounting ledgers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e431-108">Þessi skráning notar USP2-sýnigagnafyrirtæki.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e431-108">This recording uses the USP2 demo data company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="7e431-109">Fara í Kostnaðarbókhald > Fjárhags uppsetning > Kostnaðarbókhald fjárhags.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e431-109">Go to Cost accounting > Ledger setup > Cost accounting ledgers.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="7e431-110">Smellið á „Nýtt“.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e431-110">Click New.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="7e431-111">Í reitinn Heiti skal slá inn gildi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e431-111">In the Name field, type a value.</span></span>

5. <span data-ttu-id="7e431-113">Sláið inn eða veldu gildi í reitnum Fjárhagsdagatal.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e431-113">In the Fiscal calendar field, enter or select a value.</span></span>

8. <span data-ttu-id="7e431-116">Sláið inn eða veljið gildi í reitnum Tölfræðileg vídd.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e431-116">In the Statistical dimension field, enter or select a value.</span></span>
