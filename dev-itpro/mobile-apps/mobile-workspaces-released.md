"[!include[banner](../includes/banner.md)]"

| Fartækjavinnusvæði   | lýsing                                                                                                                                                                                                      | Frekari upplýsingar                                                                                                      |
|--------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
